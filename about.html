About the store

Local resale with personality, variety, and a reason to stop back in.

Weatherford Local Resale & Thrift is built around the fun part of thrift shopping: not knowing exactly what you will find, but knowing it will be worth a look. Owned by Savvy Tha Savage, the store brings together mixed resale inventory in a way that feels approachable, practical, and fun to browse.

The vibe

Not stuffy. Not overdone. Just a good local shop with a changing mix of finds.

Treasure-hunt shopping without the nonsense

Some people walk in looking for something useful. Some are hunting for décor, collectibles, or a random surprise they did not expect. The point is that the store gives people a reason to browse, not just check one shelf and leave.

Local, practical, and easy to come back to

Because the inventory rotates, the experience stays fresh. That matters for resale shoppers. When customers know a place changes often, they are more likely to stop in again instead of assuming they have already seen it all.
